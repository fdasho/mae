The Business

Fuck Olivia (Now) is a clothing brand at it's face value. However, under deeper discovery it actually is much more than just a clothing brand. It is a way of life.
The expression would be while you wearing the shirt, you are in essence, "Fucking Olivia". This is harsh again face value, but looking at the current culture, and the counter culture, it is actually right on time.

Examples :
Nike : Just Do It
Apple : Think Different
Coke : Enjoy.
F.O.N. : FUCK OLIVIA NOW.

So in essence, we will be creating commercials, advertising, and other sort of campaigns, using different social marketing platforms to sell "FON".
This also includes, festivals, events, or any other sort of practical gathering place for people to share and have "FON".

The Market

This will be very blunt, but very true, the amount of porn consumed has sky rocketed in the last 4 years. Essentially, we are looking at desensitized youth, and frankly a bored youth. They have seen everything there is see to under the sun (they think), and have no bars or limits anymore on to what they will consume or watch. TikTok is the number 1 social media platform right now for a good reason. Their attention span is short, and again, everything... even TikTok is boring to them.

There is an untapped market right now, that is slowly becoming exposed and used by larger companies, of course, through TikTok, but we are thinking much more viral, and more visual. Ergo, "FUCK OLIVIA NOW" is just 'sexy' enough to catch their attention.

Progress/Proof

As of now, I have successfully sold and marketed a small quantity of shirts (40) just as a beta test of how it would succeed in the market. Everyone wanted one. This is located in San Diego, but the potential in New York, Los Angels, or even across the Atlantic is insane.

In addition, the people who it grabbed, the people who are involved, the ones who are dedicated can not put it down. They wear the shirt constantly, they watch the videos repetitively, and are "addicted" to F.O.N. and for good reason.

F.O.N. is all about having fun, that is the premise of it's name. We capture that feeling, market it, sell it, and recreate it.

Objectives/Future

In the future, the long term goal is a digital interaction between the user and the company using artificial intelligence, and augmented reality. We want to create events, drops, and parties that are all around an application and the user participating. We would hide QR codes in cities, which would hold premium content that they would have to find, have services that we can offer, and generally provide an actual fun and interesting reason to wear the clothing.

As well as that, we want to make it more public and marketable to a larger audience. We do not hide the fact that "FON" stands for FUCK OLIVIA NOW, but we no longer actually put those words on t-shirts, or anywhere, and it is a little known wink between the consumer and the company.
